Ugandan Beef Lubombo

Banana Leaf Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1kg of beef
  • 2 Onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • garlic
  • 1 green pepper
  • irish potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • salt and pepper
  • royco
  • fresh banana leaves

Preparation

  1. Soften the banana leaves over an open flame and a roast the beef on mild heat.
  2. Mix the roasted beef with garlic, salt, pepper in a large bowl and allow it to marinate for about 30mins
  3. Take the banana leaf and place pieces of the marinated meat in the center
  4. Add a few potatoes, carrots, onions, tomatoes, green pepper and royco
  5. Wrap the banana leaves to foam a pouch, ensure the sauce doesnt leak and tie the top with a string or banana fiber
  6. Place the luwombo packets in a large pot, steam it for about 2hours until the meat and vegetables are well cooked.
  7. Serve with traditional sides like matooke, rice or posho.
