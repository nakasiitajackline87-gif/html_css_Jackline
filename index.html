Luwombo Schüssel

Traditional Banana Leaf Stew

⏱ 2 hrs 🍽 4 servings 🔥 Medium 🌿 Traditional

Popular Dishes

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef
  • 2 onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • garlic
  • 1 green pepper
  • Irish potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • salt and pepper
  • Royco
  • fresh banana leaves

Preparation

  1. Soften banana leaves over heat and lightly roast beef.
  2. Mix beef with garlic, salt, pepper and marinate 30 minutes.
  3. Place meat in banana leaf center.
  4. Add vegetables and seasoning.
  5. Wrap tightly into a pouch.
  6. Steam for 2 hours until tender.
  7. Serve with matooke, rice or posho.
